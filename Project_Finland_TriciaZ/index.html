Continent

Finland is located in Northern Europe.

Flag Origin

Designed by the artists Eero Snellman and Bruno Tuukkanen at the start of the 20th century when Finland declared its independence from Russia.The cross design symbolizes that Finland is part of the community of Nordic nations. It’s said the white symbolizes “the white Finland” and the snow that covers the country, and the blue symbolizes water in the “land of a thousand lakes”.

Happiest Country

Explained by the high levels of trust and freedom in its society – which research shows contributes to well-being and productivity. “In a well-functioning, fair and equal society, people can worry less and concentrate on living their lives.
